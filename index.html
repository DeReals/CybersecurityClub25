About Our Club

Loyola Cybersecurity Club is a student-led organization dedicated to enhancing security awareness, hands-on learning, and professional development in the field of cybersecurity. Whether you're a beginner exploring digital defense or an advanced tech enthusiast, the club provides workshops, competitions, and networking opportunities to strengthen your skills. Members engage in ethical hacking, cyber defense strategies, and discussions on emerging threats to prepare for careers in security and IT.
